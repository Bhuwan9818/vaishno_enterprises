Lamination is what separates standard plates from premium-grade ones in the market. This fully automatic machine applies a thin, even waterproof coating to finished plates, improving their durability with liquids and giving them a noticeably more polished finish. For manufacturers looking to move into higher-margin product lines or supply premium retail and event segments, this is the equipment that makes that shift possible.

Does this replace my plate-making machine?+
No, this is a separate finishing step that runs after plates are formed on your existing plate-making machine.
What type of film does it use?+
It's compatible with standard BOPP and PE lamination films widely available from packaging suppliers.
How much does lamination add to my per-plate cost?+
Film and running costs vary by supplier, but most customers find the price premium achievable in the market comfortably covers the added cost.
Can it laminate different plate sizes without changeover?+
Minor adjustments are needed when switching plate sizes; we cover this in operator training.
