    The Double Die Full Automatic Machine is engineered for small and mid-scale manufacturers who need to scale beyond a single-die setup without jumping straight to hydraulic investment. Two dies work in tandem on a single belt-driven mechanism, doubling your output per cycle while keeping the machine compact enough for a standard production room. Every unit is assembled at our Delhi facility using mild steel framing and tested under load before it ever reaches your floor.

    ⚙

    Twin-Die Output
