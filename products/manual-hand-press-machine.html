Before investing in an automatic machine, many entrepreneurs start here. The Manual Hand Press requires no electrical connection at all — just a sturdy frame, a hand-operated pressing wheel, and a single die. It's the lowest-risk way to understand the plate-making process, test your local market, and generate initial revenue before committing to a larger automatic setup.
